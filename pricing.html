Yes — your plan expires at the end of the billing period you paid for. There's no recurring auto-charge — you choose to renew when your plan expires.

Yes. Paystack is PCI-DSS compliant and processes billions in transactions across Africa. EagleEyE never sees or stores your card or Mobile Money details.
